About

Mohammad Nazeri

Got his bachelor degree in Computer Science from Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman in 2015. He is currently MSc. student of Computer Science at Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS). His primary research interests lie in autonomous agents. For autonomous agents to become prevalent in our society, they must percept, reason and forecast the future based on these observations. If they achieve this end, they can reason about human's behaviors. Currently, He is working on Trajectory Forecasting for Autonomous Vehicles as his MSc. thesis.
