About

Mohammad Nazeri

I'm a wondering researcher in the field of computer science. I periodically follow articles in neuroscience to find relations between how humans learn and how can we exert the cues of human learning in the benefit of true artificial intelligence. Overall, my research interests lie in reinforcement learning, unsupervised learning, and computational neuroscience. I got my bachelor's degree in computer science from Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman, Iran in 2015. I'm currently pursuing master's degree in computer science at Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS). I work on trajectory forecasting in autonomous vehicles (AVs) as my thesis under the supervision of Prof. Mahdi Bohlouli. If machines can understand human behavior, they can foresee future actions which are a huge help in AVs to plan better and safer paths.
